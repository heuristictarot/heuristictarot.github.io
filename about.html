About

Hi! My name is Katelyn.

I am a tarot reader based in Brooklyn, New York who specializes in readings for personal growth and development.

I believe that tarot is more of a mirror than a crystal ball— that is, it provides a new perspective rather than revealing unknown information.

As a designer and web developer, I'm passionate about creating tarot-related content that is easy to understand and nice to look at. I'm currently working on a resource guide for Adult Children of Alcoholics (ACOA), and I make educational tarot infographics for my blog.

I'm available for individual readings (in-person and online) as well as parties and events - details coming soon!

Code of ethics & my approach to tarot

Obligatory disclaimer: In certain jurisdictions, a tarot professional is required to disclose to you that tarot readings should be for entertainment purposes only.

If your inquiry involves the law, finance, or medicine, then you should seek the advice of a licensed or qualified legal, financial, or medical professional. Also, tarot readings cannot replace qualified mental health care.

That being said, I personally use tarot in conjunction with professional mental health care. Many people incorporate tarot into their spiritual and religious practices, and still others consult tarot as they would a coach or mentor. It's up to you to decide how tarot fits (or doesn't fit) into your life.

How I learned tarot

  • The Ultimate Guide to the Thoth Tarot by Johannes Fiebig and Evelin Burger
